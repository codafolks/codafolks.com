What is CodaFolks?

Hi, I’m Falconiere Barbosa, and CodaFolks is my personal side project—a space where I share the tools, projects, and insights I’ve created as a developer. This isn’t just a portfolio; it’s an open, collaborative hub for anyone passionate about software development.

CodaFolks starts as my way of sharing the projects I am building, but it’s grown into something more:

A Showcase of My Side Projects

Explore tools and software I’ve created to solve real-world problems or just for fun.

A Learning Resource

Everything (or almost) is open-source, so you can dive into the code, learn from it, and even contribute.

A Consulting Agency

Looking for custom software solutions? I offer consulting services to help turn your ideas into reality.
